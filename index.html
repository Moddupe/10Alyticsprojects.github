My Capstone Project Recruitment Analysis

The project was one of a kind. Tedsquare Ltd just recovered from pandemic effects and needs to make difficult decisions to keep business afloat. Management is cutting cost in recruiting and I was contacted as an HR Analyst to make senior managers make informed decisions. After analysis we discovered that the company can tackle the escalating recruitment costs by embracing candidates/skills-driven hiring approaches and effectively utilizing internal talents.This analysis was performed using Excel and Power BI.

Human Resource Absenteeism Analysis using Power Bi

The company Echo Media contacted us due to issues with employee absenteeism. After analysis, we discovered that for them to reduce employee absenteeism they must consider measures such as; flexible work schedules, remote work options, wellness programs, incentives for good attendance, clear communication about expectations and creating a positive work environment. We also suggested addressing underlying issues such as workload, workplace culture, and employee engagement can also help decrease absenteeism..

HR Attrition Analysis using Power BI

As an HR Analyst I was contacted to help solve the problem a company was encountering due to gradual reduction and loss of employees from the company's workforce over time. After analysis, we recommended that the company develop programs to enhance employees engagement and satisfaction eg feedback sessions and so on. Offer Compensation and Benefits, Invest in Training and Development, Provide career path opportuinities, conduct exit interviews, Recognize and reward employees for their contributions and achievements and so on. By implementing these stategies, the company can reduce attrition rates and create a more stable and engaged workforce..
